Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

MDReader does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data. Ever.

What data MDReader collects

None. MDReader does not collect any data from you or your device.

Analytics and tracking

MDReader contains no analytics SDK, no crash reporter that phones home, and no third-party tracking of any kind. There are no advertising identifiers, no fingerprinting, and no usage telemetry.

Your documents

All documents you open or create with MDReader remain exclusively on your device or in your personal iCloud Drive account. MDReader reads and writes files directly through the iOS/macOS document system. No document content is ever sent to any server controlled by the developer.

iCloud

If you choose to store documents in iCloud Drive, those files are subject to Apple's iCloud terms and privacy policy. MDReader has no access to your iCloud account credentials and does not interact with iCloud directly — the operating system handles all synchronisation.

Network access

MDReader makes no outbound network requests of its own. The Mermaid diagram renderer runs entirely on-device using a bundled JavaScript library — no external server is contacted.

Children's privacy

MDReader does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given that MDReader collects no data, meaningful changes to this policy are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact: support@thaion.dev
